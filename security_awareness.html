In today's digital age, security awareness is essential to protect yourself and your data from cyber threats. This page will guide you through essential tips and practices to stay safe online.

Quick Tips:

  1. Always use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  3. Be cautious of suspicious emails or links.
  4. Keep your software and devices up-to-date.
